City Overview

Name: Verdantia

Location: Near Malaprabha river

Time Zone: +5:30 GMT

STD code: 8312

Pin Code: 590312 – 590324 (12 post offices)

Job Creation

  • IT sector
  • Government officials
  • Renewable energy maintenance
  • Farmers
  • Corporate workers
  • Shop owners
  • Rail operators
  • Police
  • Judicial sector

Population

The initial population of the city will be around 20 lakh people, with an expected annual growth rate of 2% to 2.5% for the next few years.

Budget Summary

The proposed budget is 78.3 thousand crores for a fiscal year, with a 2% yearly increase. Deficit and surplus budgets are yet to be planned.

Revenue Model

  • Taxes
  • Public Transport Revenue
  • Grants from the central Government
  • Renewable energy revenue generation
  • Tourist hub

Sustainability Measures

  • Promote agriculture to strengthen the land
  • Make energy efficient residential areas
  • Mandate rainwater harvesting in all residencies
  • Subsidies on EV's to reduce pollution

Budget Allocation (in crores)

Sector Amount Details

Education, Sports, Arts and Culture 21000 Includes building of educational institutes, sports arenas, museums etc.
Health and Family Welfare 10000 Includes building of hospitals, pharmacies etc.
Residential 25000 Includes apartments/plots for citizens. Tenders will be given out to builders
Law and Judiciary 1500 Includes making of several police stations, district and high courts
