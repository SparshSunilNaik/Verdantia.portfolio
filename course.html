Problem Statement

Our project aims to address various issues in the railway network of Vandhya city, focusing on improving efficiency, security, and passenger experience through the application of advanced algorithms and data structures.

Causes of Problems

  • Inefficient supply chain management
  • Inadequate security measures for passenger data
  • Suboptimal route planning leading to longer travel times
  • Environmental concerns due to pollution
  • Passenger discomfort due to inefficient seat allocation
  • Ineffective train scheduling causing delays
  • Unfair online booking systems

Method of Evaluation

To select the final business cases, we used the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP), a structured technique for organizing and analyzing complex decisions. The process involved:

  • Defining criteria: Impact, feasibility, cost-effectiveness, and innovation
  • Pairwise comparison of criteria
  • Pairwise comparison of alternatives for each criterion
  • Calculating global priorities
  • Selecting the top-ranking alternatives

This method helped us objectively evaluate and prioritize our ideas, leading to the selection of the seven business cases presented in the Business section.

Selected Cases

  • Improved supply chain network
  • Enhancement of Security
  • Route planning
  • Reduce pollution
  • Improve passenger comfort
  • Scheduling of trains
  • Online Booking Management

Brainstorming

    Our team brainstormed various ideas to address these issues. Some of the ideas we considered include:

  • Implementing AI-based predictive maintenance
  • Developing a blockchain-based ticketing system
  • Creating a real-time passenger information system
  • Designing an IoT-based track monitoring system
  • Implementing a dynamic pricing model
  • Developing a mobile app for seamless passenger experience
  • Creating a centralized database for all railway operations
